Taiwan dumplings

Description

Taiwan dumplings also known as Xiao Long Bao is similar to a bun with meat wrapped inside. Though there is a very big difference between them, their structure is very similar. On the outside, the skin is made up of a thin layer of special dough made from flour while the inside is made of some sort of fatty meat, mostly it's pork. Then they are placed into a small bamboo steamer to be steamed. When steaming, the dumplings release a fragrant soup that explodes in your mouth when you eat it.

Pearl Milk Tea

Description

Pearl Milk Tea or zhen zhu nai cha is a worldwide drink. It's made up of milk, tea, and chewy tapioca pearls. This drink can be served either hot, cold, or warm depending on how you like it. There also are a lot of different kinds of milk tea such as taro milk tea but the most common one is brown sugar.
