Taipei 101

Description

Taipei 101 is a skyscraper which is 101 stories high. The name of this building came from the city of Taipei and the amount of stories it has. This skyscraper was designed by C.Y. Lee & Partners and the height if including the spire is 1,667 feet which is 508 meters tall! Taipei 101 is considered the tallest building in the whole Taiwan and is the 11th tallest building ever created in the whole world.

The Chiang Kai-shek Memorial Hall is a national monument and tourist attraction erected in memory of Chiang Kai-shek, former President of Taiwan. The architecture of this monument got their inspiration from the Temple of Heaven or Tiantan in Beijing. This monument was an important historic venue for witnessing the democratic development of Taiwan.

Sun Moon Lake

Description

The Sun Moon Lake is a very beautiful alpine lake located in Taiwan's Yuchi Township, Nantou Country. In the middle of the lake, there's an island called the Lalu Island which separates the lake into two parts. One shaped as a crescent moon while the other part is shaped like a round sun. Hence the name Sun Moon Lake.
